ABOUT THE
CLUB NYMBOUR

Nambour-Yandina United Football Club (NYU) was formed in 1997 through the merger of the Nambour Reds and the Yandina Eagles. The club offers opportunities for players of all ages, genders, and abilities in a safe and supportive environment. With over 250 players participating across various age groups and divisions. NYU is recognized throughout the Sunshine Coast not only for its strength on the field but also for its passionate community of supporters.
